# Satellite and Television Broadcast Rights

## Primary Network Partners
[cite_start]YRF licenses its catalog and new releases to India's major TV networks for exclusive broadcast windows[cite: 1725]:
- [cite_start]**Sony Entertainment Television**: Long-term historical partner[cite: 1726].
- [cite_start]**Star Network (STAR India)**: Significant licensing partner[cite: 1727].
- [cite_start]**Zee Entertainment** & **Viacom18**[cite: 1728, 1729].

## Deal Economics
- [cite_start]**Pricing**: Satellite rights typically range from ₹20–60 crore per film[cite: 1732].
- [cite_start]**Historical Peak**: *Dhoom 3* set a record in 2013 by selling satellite rights for ₹75 crore[cite: 1733].
- [cite_start]**License Terms**: Rights are usually sold for an exclusive period of 5 to 10 years[cite: 1731].

## Revenue Role
[cite_start]Satellite rights contribute roughly 15–25% of the total revenue for a standard YRF production[cite: 1435].
