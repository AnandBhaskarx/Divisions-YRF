# International Distribution and Global Reach

## Global Office Locations
[cite_start]The international division operates its own offices in three critical global hubs to maintain direct control over overseas revenue[cite: 1632, 1633]:
- [cite_start]**United Kingdom (London)**: Covers the UK and broader European markets[cite: 1634].
- [cite_start]**United States (Beverly Hills)**: Headed by YRF Entertainment for North America[cite: 1635].
- [cite_start]**United Arab Emirates (Dubai)**: Covers the Gulf and MENA regions[cite: 1636].

## Market Tiering
### Tier 1 (High Revenue/Diaspora-Rich)
- [cite_start]**Gulf Region**: UAE, Saudi Arabia, Oman, Qatar[cite: 1639].
- [cite_start]**North America**: USA and Canada[cite: 1641].
- [cite_start]**Commonwealth**: UK and Australia[cite: 1640, 1642].

### Tier 2 (Growth Markets)
- [cite_start]**Europe**: Germany, Netherlands, and Belgium[cite: 1644].
- [cite_start]**Southeast Asia**: Malaysia and Singapore[cite: 1645].
- [cite_start]**Others**: South Africa and New Zealand[cite: 1647, 1648].

## Performance Metrics
- [cite_start]**Revenue Contribution**: Overseas markets typically contribute 15–30% of a film's total box office[cite: 1653].
- [cite_start]**Benchmark**: *Pathaan* earned approximately ₹250 crore from international markets alone[cite: 1654].
