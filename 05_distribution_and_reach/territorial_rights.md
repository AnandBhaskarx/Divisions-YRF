# Territorial and Ancillary Rights Monetization

## Rights Silos
[cite_start]YRF monetizes its intellectual property (IP) across several distinct territorial and functional rights:
- [cite_start]**Theatrical Rights**: Domestic and International[cite: 1374].
- **Non-Theatrical Rights**:
    - [cite_start]**Broadcast**: Satellite and Cable TV[cite: 1419, 1715].
    - [cite_start]**Digital**: SVOD (Streaming), TVOD (Transaction), and AVOD (Ad-supported)[cite: 1420].
    - [cite_start]**Public Performance**: Ground events, hotels, and restaurants via authorized agents[cite: 1408].
    - [cite_start]**Music Rights**: Master recording and synchronization rights[cite: 1735, 1743].

## Licensing Repertoire
[cite_start]The YRF Licensing division manages a 50+ year catalog including[cite: 1412]:
- [cite_start]**Film Titles & Logos**: Spy Universe and Dhoom franchise IP[cite: 1414, 1415].
- [cite_start]**Music**: Rights to iconic soundtracks like *DDLJ* and *Dil To Pagal Hai*[cite: 1413].
- [cite_start]**Clips & Dialogue**: Licensing for TV, advertisements, and educational use[cite: 1887, 1895].

## Revenue Contribution
- [cite_start]**Music Rights**: 5–10% of total film revenue[cite: 1437].
- [cite_start]**Licensing/Merchandise**: 2–5%[cite: 1439].
