# YRF Quality Control & Assurance Processes

## Executive Summary
YRF follows a multi-layered quality control framework covering creative, technical, and compliance standards across the production lifecycle.

## Creative Quality Gates
1. Script approval
2. Casting validation
3. Production design sign-off
4. Editorial lock
5. Final sound and color approval

## Technical Standards
- Minimum 4K capture
- Broadcast and theatrical color compliance
- Audio mastering to international standards

## Review Mechanisms
- Daily dailies review
- Weekly production audits
- Post-production review cycles

## Compliance Verification
- Legal clearance
- Safety audits
- Certification readiness

## Continuous Improvement
- Post-project audits
- Lessons-learned documentation
- Workflow optimization updates
