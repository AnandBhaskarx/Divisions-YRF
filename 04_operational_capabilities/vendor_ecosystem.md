# YRF Vendor Ecosystem & Partner Management

## Executive Summary
YRF operates a curated ecosystem of approved vendors and service partners to ensure reliability, quality, and scalability across productions.

## Vendor Categories
- Equipment rental
- VFX & post-production
- Catering & logistics
- Transport & accommodation

## Vendor Selection Criteria
- Industry experience (minimum benchmarks)
- Quality and reliability history
- Compliance with safety and legal standards

## Engagement Model
- Milestone-based delivery
- Performance monitoring
- Long-term preferred partnerships

## Evaluation & Renewal
- Periodic performance audits
- Cost and delivery assessment
- Compliance review
