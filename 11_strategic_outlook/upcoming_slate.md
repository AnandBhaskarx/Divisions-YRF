## Section Title
Production Pipeline & Upcoming Releases (2025–2026)

## Confirmed Theatrical Slate
YRF has shifted from a volume-based strategy to a "Quality Over Quantity" approach, focusing on 2–4 major event films per year.

- **War 2 (August 14, 2025)**:
  - **Director**: Ayan Mukerji.
  - **Cast**: Hrithik Roshan, Jr. NTR.
  - **Significance**: The sixth installment in the Spy Universe, projected as a massive pan-India revenue driver.
- **Alpha (April 2026)**:
  - **Director**: Shiv Rawail.
  - **Cast**: Alia Bhatt, Sharvari, Bobby Deol.
  - **Significance**: The first female-led film in the Spy Universe.
- **Dhoom 4 (Reboot)**:
  - **Status**: Pre-production; scheduled to go on floors April 2026.
  - **Cast**: Ranbir Kapoor (confirmed lead).
- **Mardaani 3**:
  - **Cast**: Rani Mukerji returning.
  - **Theme**: Darker, procedural chapter focused on female-led narratives.

## Strategic Streaming Content
- **Netflix Originals**: Following the success of *The Railway Men*, the studio is developing 4–6 premium limited series to capitalize on global OTT demand.
