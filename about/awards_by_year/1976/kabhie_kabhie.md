## Kabhi Kabhie (1976)
**Tagline:** Love Is Life  

### Filmfare Awards – 1976

- **Best Music** – Khayyam  
- **Best Lyricist** – Sahir Ludhianvi *(Kabhi Kabhie)*  
- **Best Playback Singer (Male)** – Mukesh *(Kabhi Kabhie)*  
- **Best Dialogue Writer** – Sagar Sarhadi  

### Significance
*Kabhi Kabhie* reaffirmed Yash Chopra’s mastery over large-scale romantic dramas at a time when such films were considered commercially risky. The film’s poetry-driven narrative, soulful music, and layered emotions redefined the romantic genre in Hindi cinema and established the film as a timeless classic.
