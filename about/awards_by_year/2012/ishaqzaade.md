# Ishaqzaade (2012)

## National Film Awards
- **Special Mention** – Parineeti Chopra

- **Best Playback Singer (Male)** – Javed Ali (*Ishaqzaade – Title Track*)  
- **Best Playback Singer (Female)** – Shalmali Kholgade (*Pareshaan*)

## Filmfare Awards
- **Best Playback Singer (Female)** – Shalmali Kholgade (*Pareshaan*)

- **Best Debut (Male)** – Arjun Kapoor *(jointly with Ayushmann Khurrana)*

- **Sa Re Ga Ma Pa Fresh Singing Talent** – Shalmali Kholgade (*Pareshaan*)

- **Superstar of Tomorrow (Male)** – Arjun Kapoor

## Star Guild Awards
- **Best Playback Singer (Female)** – Shalmali Kholgade (*Pareshaan*)

## People’s Choice Awards
- **Favourite Debut Actor / Actress** – Parineeti Chopra
