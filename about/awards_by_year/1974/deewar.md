# Deewar – 1974

## Filmfare Awards
- Best Film – Gulshan Rai (Producer)
- Best Director – Yash Chopra
- Best Actor in a Parallel Role – Shashi Kapoor
- Best Story Writer – Salim–Javed
- Best Sound Recordist – M. A. Shaikh
- Best Dialogue Writer – Salim–Javed
- Best Screenplay Writer – Salim–Javed
