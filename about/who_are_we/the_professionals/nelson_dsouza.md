## Nelson Dsouza
Section Title
Nelson D’Souza – Vice President, International Distribution

Role Overview
Nelson D’Souza serves as Vice President – International Distribution at Yash Raj Films, bringing over three decades of experience across global entertainment markets. He leads YRF’s international theatrical, television, and home-video distribution strategy, with a focus on expanding the studio’s global footprint.

## International Distribution Leadership at YRF
Nelson joined YRF in 2005 as **Senior Manager – UAE & GCC Operations**. Since then, he has been instrumental in building YRF’s international distribution networks across the MENA region and other key global markets.

Key responsibilities include:
- International theatrical distribution and release strategy  
- Expansion into TV and home-video markets abroad  
- Building and managing overseas distribution networks  
- Positioning Indian cinema within mainstream multiplex ecosystems in diaspora markets  

## Market Expansion and Brand Building
During his tenure, Nelson developed long-standing relationships in markets traditionally dominated by Hollywood distributors. His efforts helped:
- Establish a strong YRF brand presence overseas  
- Bring Indian cinema to wider, mainstream international audiences  
- Strengthen YRF’s positioning in key diaspora territories  

He has also executed innovative marketing and PR initiatives in collaboration with:
- Local radio and television networks  
- Multinational corporations  
- Government bodies  
- Exhibition chains  

These partnerships enhanced visibility and audience engagement for YRF releases worldwide.

## Professional Background Prior to YRF
Before joining YRF, Nelson D’Souza began his career with **BMG (Bertelsmann Music Group)** in India. Over a 10-year tenure, he rose to the position of **Director of Sales**, gaining deep exposure to the commercial and distribution dynamics of music and entertainment businesses.

Strategic Value to the Studio
Nelson D’Souza contributes:
- Deep understanding of international cinema markets  
- Strong global partner relationships  
- Expertise in navigating competitive, Hollywood-dominated territories  
- Long-term vision for sustainable overseas growth  

His experience and market insight continue to strengthen YRF’s global distribution network.
