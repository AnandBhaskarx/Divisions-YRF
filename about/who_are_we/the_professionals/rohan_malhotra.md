## RohanMalhotra
Section Title
Rohan Malhotra – Vice President, Distribution

Role Overview
Rohan Malhotra serves as Vice President – Distribution at Yash Raj Films, leading the studio’s theatrical distribution business across India, Nepal, and Bhutan. He is responsible for end-to-end domestic release strategy, market positioning, and box-office performance management.

## Distribution Leadership at YRF
In his current role, Rohan Malhotra oversees:
- Theatrical distribution and release planning  
- Strategic dating of film releases for domestic and global markets  
- Coordination with YRF’s domestic offices and affiliate network  
- Competitive positioning of films at release  
- Tracking box-office collections, rentals, and performance metrics  
- Exhibitor and trade relations  
- Monitoring market data, trends, and content performance  
- Domestic on-ground anti-piracy initiatives  

His work ensures that YRF’s films achieve optimal reach, timing, and commercial impact.

## Career Progression at YRF
Rohan Malhotra began his association with YRF in **May 2006** as a summer intern in the Mumbai regional distribution office. Following strong performance, he was appointed **Head of Operations – Mumbai Distribution** in April 2007.

His career progression reflects consistent responsibility expansion:
- **2007:** Head of Operations – Mumbai Distribution  
- **2011:** General Manager – Distribution (All-India responsibilities)  
- **Present:** Vice President – Distribution  

Over more than a decade at YRF, he has risen through the ranks through sustained performance and deep operational expertise.

## Strategic Value to the Studio
Rohan Malhotra brings:
- Strong on-ground understanding of domestic exhibition markets  
- Data-driven release and performance tracking  
- Long-term exhibitor and trade relationships  
- Alignment between creative output and commercial execution  

His leadership ensures disciplined, market-aware distribution strategies that support YRF’s studio-scale releases.
