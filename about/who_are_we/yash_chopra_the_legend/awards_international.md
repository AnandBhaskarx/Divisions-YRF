## Section Title
Yash Chopra – International Honours & Recognitions

Overview
He stands among the most internationally honoured filmmakers in Indian cinema history. His work transcended national boundaries, earning recognition from global film institutions, governments, cultural bodies, and international festivals. These honours reflect both artistic excellence and Yash Chopra’s role as a cultural ambassador of Indian cinema worldwide.

---

## Global Film & Media Awards

- **BBC Asia Award** (1998, 2001)  
  Yash Chopra was the only Indian filmmaker to receive this award twice, recognising his outstanding contribution to cinema.

- **IIFA – Outstanding Contribution to Indian Cinema** (2002, Malaysia)

- **Asian Guild Award** (2003, London)

- **Lifetime Achievement Award**, CineAsia (2004, Bangkok)  
  He was the **first Indian filmmaker ever** to be honoured at CineAsia.

- **Variety Cinema Personality of the Year** (2005)  
  Awarded by *Variety* magazine for his indelible impact on world cinema.

- **FIAPF Award for Outstanding Achievement in Film** (2008, Australia)  
  Presented at the Asia Pacific Screen Awards.

- **Asian Filmmaker of the Year**, Pusan International Film Festival (2009)

- **Asian Award for Outstanding Achievement in Cinema** (2010, London)

---

## International Film Festivals & Global Screenings

- **Berlin International Film Festival**  
  - *Veer-Zaara* screened at the **55th Berlinale (2005)**  
  - Yash Chopra served as a **jury member** at the **56th Berlinale (2006)** — the first Bollywood personality in 25 years to receive this honour

- **Dubai International Film Festival** (2005)  
  Honoured alongside Morgan Freeman and Adel Imam in the *In the Spotlight* programme

- **Marrakech International Film Festival** (2005, 2012)  
  Honoured by HRH Prince Moulay Rachid; *Veer-Zaara* and *Jab Tak Hai Jaan* received standing ovations

- **Grand Rex Cinema, Paris** (2006)  
  *Veer-Zaara* became the **first Indian film** to premiere at the iconic Grand Rex

- **Hawaii International Film Festival** (2005)

- **Jeonju International Film Festival, South Korea** (2006)

- **Doha Tribeca Film Festival** (2012)  
  *Jab Tak Hai Jaan* received a standing ovation

---

## International Academic & Cultural Recognition

- **British Film Institute (BFI)**  
  Featured Yash Chopra in its prestigious *World Directors Series*.  
  The book *Yash Chopra* by Dr. Rachel Dwyer was launched in London, New Delhi, and Mumbai.

- **Honorary Doctorates**
  - Leeds Metropolitan University, UK (2007)
  - SOAS – University of London (Doctor of Letters, 2010)

- **BAFTA Honour (2006)**  
  First Indian filmmaker honoured in BAFTA’s 59-year history.  
  BAFTA curated a **three-day Indian Cinema showcase**, headlined by *Veer-Zaara*.

- **Channel 4 (UK)**  
  Dedicated a special film festival titled *The World of Yash Chopra* (2006).

---

## International Government & Cultural Honours

- **Officier de la Légion d’Honneur** (France, 2008)  
  France’s highest civilian honour, bestowed by decree of President Nicolas Sarkozy.  
  Yash Chopra joined an elite list including Satyajit Ray, Ravi Shankar, Lata Mangeshkar, and Amitabh Bachchan.

- **Honoured at the UK Parliament** (House of Lords & House of Commons, 2007)

- **Swiss Ambassador’s Award** (2010)  
  For promoting Switzerland globally through cinema.

- **Ambassador of Interlaken** (2011)  
  First recipient of this honour.
