## Section Title
Yash Chopra – National Honours & Recognitions

## Overview
For his pioneering and transformative contributions to Indian cinema, :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0} received virtually every major honour and recognition bestowed by the Government of India, national institutions, industry bodies, academic organizations, and cultural forums. These recognitions reflect not only cinematic excellence but also his leadership in shaping the Indian entertainment ecosystem.

---

## National & Government Honours

Dadasaheb Phalke Award
Yash Chopra was selected for the **prestigious Dadasaheb Phalke Award**, the highest honour conferred by the Government of India in the field of cinema, recognising his lifetime achievement and unparalleled contribution to Indian filmmaking.

Padma Bhushan (2005)
He was conferred the **Padma Bhushan**, one of India’s highest civilian honours, by the Honourable President of India, **Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam**, for his outstanding service to Indian culture and cinema.

## Industry Leadership & Institutional Roles

- **Founder Trustee**, Film Industry Welfare Trust (established in 1996)
- **Chairman**, Entertainment Committee of FICCI (Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce & Industry) in 2002
- **Advisory Board Member**, Ministry of Information & Broadcasting, Government of India

These roles reflected Yash Chopra’s influence beyond filmmaking, positioning him as a key voice in policy, welfare, and industry development.

## Posthumous Honours & Tributes (2012–2013)

Following his passing on 21 October 2012, Yash Chopra’s legacy was celebrated nationally and internationally:

- **NDTV Indian of the Year Award (Posthumous)** – 2013  
  Presented by former Prime Minister **Dr. Manmohan Singh** to Pamela Chopra

- **Filmfare Lifetime Achievement Award** – 2013  
  Filmfare also announced the **Yash Chopra Award for Best Debut Director**, instituted in his honour

- **Screen Lifetime Achievement Award** – 2013  
  Accepted by Pamela Chopra on his behalf

- **Statue Unveiled** at UTV Stars’ Walk of the Stars, Bandra Bandstand (2013)

- **Commemorative Postal Stamp** released by **India Post** on the occasion of 100 Years of Indian Cinema (2013)

- Nationwide tributes at major award ceremonies including **Filmfare** and **Zee Cine Awards**

## Public & Cultural Legacy

- Declared the **Greatest Director in Hindi Cinema** in a nationwide Filmfare poll conducted to commemorate **100 Years of Indian Cinema**
