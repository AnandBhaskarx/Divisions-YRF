## YRF Milestones – 2018

### Digital & Music Records
- **16 November 2018**: The trailer of **:contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}** became the **first Indian film trailer to cross 100 million views on YouTube**.
- **7 July 2018**: **“Swag Se Swagat”** became the **first Indian song to cross 500 million views on YouTube**.
- **“Nashe Si Chadh Gayi”** became the **first Hindi song** to cross **350 million** and **400 million views** on YouTube.
- **“Swag Se Swagat”** became the **fastest Bollywood song** to clock **200M, 300M and 400M views**.
- **9 May 2018**: **“Nashe Si Chadh Gayi”** and **“Swag Se Swagat”** became the **Top 2 most viewed Hindi songs on YouTube**.
