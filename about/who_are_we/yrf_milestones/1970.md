## YRF Milestones – 1970

### Foundation
- **Yash Raj Films (YRF)** was founded in **1970** by **Yash Chopra** as an independent film-making company.
- The formation of YRF marked the beginning of a studio that would go on to **reshape Hindi cinema**, spanning storytelling, music, talent creation, global distribution, and studio-led filmmaking.

### Significance
- This year laid the foundation for what would become **India’s most influential and enduring film studio**, with a legacy spanning multiple generations of cinema.
