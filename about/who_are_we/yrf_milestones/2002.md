## YRF Milestones – 2002

### Cinema
- **20 December 2002**: **:contentReference[oaicite:12]{index=12}** released.
  - Directed by **Shaad Ali**
  - Written by **Mani Ratnam**
  - Music by **A.R. Rahman**

### Quality & Innovation
- YRF received the **Golden Peacock National Quality Award** for excellence in Total Quality Management.
- Among the first Indian studios to deploy film content across the **mobile ecosystem**.

### Global Recognition – DDLJ
- The **:contentReference[oaicite:13]{index=13}** commissioned a book on **:contentReference[oaicite:14]{index=14}** under the **BFI Modern Classics** series.
- First Hindi film to be included, recognising its **global cultural impact**.

### Switzerland Honour
- The **Swiss Filmfare Award** was instituted and presented to **Yash Chopra** for promoting Switzerland as a global tourism destination through cinema.
