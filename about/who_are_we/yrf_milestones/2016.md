## YRF Milestones – 2016

### Studio & Technology
- **February 2016**: YRF Studios inaugurated its **in-house VFX division – yFX Studios**, establishing a full end-to-end filmmaking ecosystem.
- **SULTAN** became the **first film** to be worked on by **yFX Studios**.

### International Recognition
- **May 2016**: The **Swiss Government** honoured **Yash Chopra** with a **bronze statue** in Interlaken, Switzerland, recognising his contribution to promoting Brand Switzerland through cinema.

### Box Office
- **6 July 2016**: **:contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}** released worldwide.
  - Became the **third highest-grossing Hindi film of all time**.
  - Broke **35+ box-office records** globally.
