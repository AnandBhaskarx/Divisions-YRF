## YRF Milestones – 2012

### Cinema Releases
- **11 May 2012**: **:contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}** released.
  - Directed by **Habib Faisal**
  - Introduced **Arjun Kapoor**
  - **Parineeti Chopra** won the **National Film Award**

- **15 August 2012**: **:contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}** released.
  - Marked YRF’s **first collaboration with Salman Khan**
  - Directed by **Kabir Khan**

- **13 November 2012**: **:contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}** released.
  - Yash Chopra’s **final directorial film**
  - First collaboration with **A.R. Rahman** (music) and **Gulzar** (lyrics)

### Legacy
- On his **80th birthday**, **Yash Chopra** announced **Jab Tak Hai Jaan** as his final film.
- He passed away on **21 October 2012**, marking the end of an era in Indian cinema.

### Innovation & IP
- **July 2012**: YRF launched **YOMICS**, its digital comics division, extending film IP into new storytelling formats.
