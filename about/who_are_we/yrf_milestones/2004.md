## YRF Milestones – 2004

### Cinema
- **28 May 2004**: **:contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}** released.
  - Directed by **Kunal Kohli**
  - **Saif Ali Khan** won the **National Award for Best Actor**
- **27 August 2004**: **:contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}** released.
  - Became a **runaway blockbuster**
  - Launched the iconic **DHOOM franchise**
- **12 November 2004**: **:contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}** released.
  - Won **National Award for Best Film – Popular & Wholesome Entertainment**
  - Celebrated for music, storytelling and cross-border theme

### Music & Distribution
- **YRF Music** launched internationally with **Dhoom** and **Hum Tum**.
- A full-fledged **Music & Home Entertainment Division** was established for global distribution.
- YRF began **domestic and international theatrical distribution** for other production houses.

### Global Industry Standing
- **:contentReference[oaicite:11]{index=11}** ranked YRF among the **world’s biggest film production houses**, alongside major Hollywood studios.
- YRF ranked **27th globally**, becoming **India’s #1 distribution house**.
