## YRF Milestones – 2006

### Cinema
- **26 May 2006**: **:contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}** released.
  - Marked **Aamir Khan’s first YRF film**
- **24 November 2006**: **:contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}** released.
  - Widest release in Indian cinema history at the time (1800+ prints)
  - Highest-grossing Hindi film of 2006
- **15 December 2006**: **:contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}** released.
  - Launched **Kabir Khan** as a director
  - Won **National Award for Best Debut Film of a Director**

### Technology & Merchandising
- YRF launched **YRF Merchandise** with **Fanaa**.
- **9 November 2006**: Microsoft CEO **:contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}** and **Yash Chopra** launched the DRM-enabled YRF website, pioneering digital media consumption in Indian entertainment.
