## YRF Milestones – 2020

### Iconic Film Anniversary
- **20 October 2020**: **:contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}**, Aditya Chopra’s directorial debut, completed **25 years**, remaining the longest-running film in Indian cinema.

- **27 September 2020**: **Yash Raj Films** completed **50 years** of entertaining audiences worldwide, marking five decades of cinematic excellence.

- **22 April 2020**: YRF’s YouTube channel crossed **30 million subscribers**.
- **19 April 2020**: YRF’s Facebook page crossed **15 million followers**.
