## YRF Milestones – 2013

### Fashion & Brand Extension
- YRF partnered with **Karol Bagh Saree House (KBSH)** to launch **Diva’ni**, India’s first Bollywood-inspired fashion label.

### Cinema & Technology
- **20 December 2013**: **:contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}** released.
  - First Indian film released in **IMAX format** with **Dolby Atmos**
  - Grossed **INR 556 Crores worldwide**
  - Declared **Biggest Hit of 2013** by Box Office India
