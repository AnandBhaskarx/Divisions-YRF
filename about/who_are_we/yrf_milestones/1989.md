## YRF Milestones – 1989

### Cinema
- **14 September 1989**: **:contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}** released.
  - Revived the era of **romantic musicals** at a time dominated by action cinema
  - **Sridevi’s white costumes** became iconic as the **“Chandni Look”**
  - Songs became nationwide chartbusters

### Cultural Impact
- Extensive filming in **Switzerland** helped popularize it as a **tourist destination for Indian travellers**.

### National Recognition
- **Chandni** won the **National Award for Best Film – Popular & Wholesome Entertainment**.
