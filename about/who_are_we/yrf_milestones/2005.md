## YRF Milestones – 2005

### Infrastructure
- **12 October 2005 (Dussehra)**: YRF moved into its **new corporate headquarters and fully integrated film studio**.

### Industry Recognition
- YRF won **Best Production Company** and **Best Marketing Company** at the **Apsara Film & Television Producers Guild Awards 2005**.
- **Yash Chopra** received the **Superbrand Award** from Superbrands International.
- Yash Chopra and Aditya Chopra were ranked the **most powerful figures** in Indian entertainment.
- YRF secured the **#1 position in Filmfare’s Power List 2005**, surpassing individual superstars.
