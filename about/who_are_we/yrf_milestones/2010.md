## YRF Milestones – 2010

### Cinema
- **10 December 2010**: **:contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}** released.
  - Directed by debutant **Maneesh Sharma**
  - Introduced **Ranveer Singh**
  - Became a **critical and commercial success** and one of the **most awarded films of the year**

### Television
- **January 2010**: YRF entered television with **YRF Television**, producing new-age content for **Sony Entertainment Television**, reshaping contemporary TV storytelling.

### Global Brand Collaboration
- YRF partnered with **SOTC** and Swiss tourism stakeholders to launch **“Yash Raj Films’ Enchanted Journey”**, offering Indian film fans a customized cinematic travel experience in Switzerland.

### International Recognition – Yash Chopra
- **Yash Chopra** was honoured as **‘Ambassador of Interlaken’**, becoming the **first recipient** of the title.
- A **silver plaque** was installed on **Hoheweg**, Interlaken’s main street.
- Additional tributes included:
  - **Yash Chopra Suite** at Victoria Jungfrau Grand Hotel & Spa
  - **Yash Chopra Train** launched as part of Jungfrau Railways
