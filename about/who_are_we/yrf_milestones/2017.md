## YRF Milestones – 2017

### Industry Recognition
- Yash Raj Films won **National Producer of the Year (Corporate)** at the **International Film Business Awards (IFBA) 2017**, organised by Indywood Film Carnival, Hyderabad.

### Digital Records – TIGER ZINDA HAI
- **“Swag Se Swagat”** became the **most liked Bollywood song on YouTube** with **1M+ likes**.
- **:contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}** trailer became the **first Hindi movie trailer** to receive **1M likes**.
- **“Swag Se Swagat”** clocked **1M streams in 24 hours**.
- **“Swag Se Swagat”** ranked **#1 song in India for two consecutive weeks**.
- **“Dil Diyan Gallan”** ranked **#1 song in India for seven consecutive weeks**.
- **“Dil Diyan Gallan”** became the **most streamed Bollywood song in 24 hours** with **1.5M streams**.
- **650K+ tweets**: most tweeted Hindi movie trailer in 24 hours.
- **250K+ shares**: most shared Hindi movie trailer in 24 hours.
- **29M+ views**: most viewed Hindi movie trailer in 24 hours (YouTube + Facebook).

### International Awards – SULTAN
- **:contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}** won top honours at the **Tehran International Sports Film Festival**:
  - **Best Actor**: Salman Khan  
  - **Best Actress**: Anushka Sharma  
  - **Best Director**: Ali Abbas Zafar
- **SULTAN** also won **Best Action Movie** at the **Shanghai International Film Festival**.
