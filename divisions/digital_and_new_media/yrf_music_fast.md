## YRF Music FAST Channel – Overview

## Channel Definition
YRF Music FAST is a Free Ad-Supported Streaming TV (FAST) channel operated by Yash Raj Films. It is a linear, round-the-clock streaming feed designed to deliver curated music and promotional content to global audiences without subscription barriers.

## Content Programming
The channel features a thematic mix of:
- Bespoke music programming  
- Hindi cinema-focused content  
- Artist-centric shows  
- Genre- and mood-specific playlists  

All programming is carefully curated to appeal to diverse audience segments, including music enthusiasts and Hindi cinema lovers across geographies.

## Viewer Experience
The music selection and programme scheduling are designed to ensure:
- Continuous entertainment  
- High recall value of YRF’s music catalogue  
- A consistent, engaging viewing experience across platforms  

The FAST format enables passive, lean-back consumption similar to traditional television, while leveraging digital scalability.

## Global Distribution Platforms
YRF Music FAST Channel is available across multiple domestic and international platforms, including:
- Jio TV (India)  
- Jio TV+ (India)  
- LG Channels (India)  
- Xiaomi TV+ (India)  
- DistroTV (India & North America)  
- Astro Sooka (Malaysia)  
- Netgem TV (UK & Europe)  
- Rewarded TV (North America, UK & Europe)  
- Amasian TV (North America & LATAM)  
- Sling TV (USA)  
- KableOne (Worldwide)  

## Strategic Importance
The YRF Music FAST Channel extends the studio’s digital music ecosystem by:
- Expanding global reach of YRF’s music library  
- Creating incremental ad-supported revenue streams  
- Reinforcing YRF’s leadership in digital content distribution  

The channel represents YRF’s continued commitment to innovation within emerging digital consumption models.
