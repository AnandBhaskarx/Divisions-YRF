## Tahir Raj Bhasin – Profile

## Career Overview
Tahir Raj Bhasin is a versatile and compelling actor known for his powerful performances across films, web series, and music videos. He has built a reputation for choosing unconventional, performance-driven roles and carving a distinct space in the industry as a leading outsider.

## Entry into YRF and Early Impact
Tahir was inducted into the YRF talent roster following his striking debut in **MARDAANI (2014)**, where his performance made a lasting impression. His association with YRF marked the beginning of a career defined by bold choices and layered characters.

## Range and Filmography
Over the years, Tahir has demonstrated exceptional range through projects such as:
- **CHHICHHORE (2019)**
- **’83 (2021)**
- **MANTO (2018)**
- **YEH KAALI KAALI ANKHEIN (2022–2024)**
- **LOOOP LAPETA (2022)**

His work reflects a consistent preference for stories that challenge norms and explore human complexity.

## Recent Work and Critical Acclaim
Tahir received widespread acclaim for his role as the antagonist **Sudheer Awasthi aka Collector** in **SPECIAL OPS SEASON 2 (2025)**, where his performance was highlighted as one of the standout elements of the series.

He continues to expand his presence in the streaming space and is currently working on an upcoming mystery thriller series by **Siddharth P. Malhotra**, alongside preparations for **YEH KAALI KAALI ANKHEIN SEASON 3**.

## Personal Ethos
A self-professed adventure enthusiast, Tahir brings curiosity, discipline, and depth to his craft. His journey stands as a strong example of how talent and persistence can build a lasting career without industry backing.
