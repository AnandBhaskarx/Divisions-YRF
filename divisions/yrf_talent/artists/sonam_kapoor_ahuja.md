## Sonam Kapoor Ahuja – Profile

## Career Overview
Sonam Kapoor Ahuja is a National Award-winning actress and a global fashion icon who has built a distinctive career through unconventional film choices and cultural influence. Over nearly two decades, she has balanced commercial cinema with meaningful, women-centric storytelling.

She is also India’s first **South Asian Global Ambassador for Dior**, reinforcing her international stature.

## Entry into Cinema
Sonam began her career assisting filmmaker Sanjay Leela Bhansali on **BLACK (2005)** before making her acting debut in **SAAWARIYA (2007)**. She gained recognition for her sensitivity and screen presence early in her career.

## Artistic Growth and Acclaimed Performances
Her career-defining performance came with **RAANJHANAA (2013)**, earning her widespread critical acclaim. She followed this with powerful performances in **BHAAG MILKHA BHAAG (2013)** and commercial successes like **PREM RATAN DHAN PAYO (2015)**.

The landmark film **NEERJA (2016)**, based on the life of Neerja Bhanot, earned Sonam a **National Film Award Special Mention** and the **Filmfare Best Actress (Critics)** award. The film became one of the most successful female-led films in Indian cinema.

## Producer and Contemporary Cinema Advocate
Along with her sister Rhea Kapoor, Sonam co-produced films such as **AISHA (2010)**, **KHOOBSURAT (2014)** and **VEERE DI WEDDING (2018)**, contributing to modern, urban storytelling in Hindi cinema.

## Fashion, Culture and Global Presence
Widely regarded as India’s most fashionable star, Sonam has represented India at global fashion events including the **Cannes Film Festival** and international royal ceremonies. She is a member of the **South Asia Acquisition Committee at Tate Modern**, becoming the only Indian actor inducted for this role.

## Social Advocacy
Sonam actively supports LGBTQ+ rights, breast cancer awareness, animal welfare (with PETA), and the arts. She is also an avid art collector and patron of South Asian artists.

## Personal Note and Future Work
After a hiatus following her marriage to Anand Ahuja and motherhood in 2022, Sonam is preparing to return with two major projects currently in development.
