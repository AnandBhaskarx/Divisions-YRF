## Sharvari – Profile

## Career Overview
Sharvari emerged as one of the most promising new actors of her generation, becoming the breakout star of 2024. With a combination of box-office success, critical acclaim, and strong digital presence, she has quickly positioned herself as a versatile and high-impact performer.

## Breakthrough and Recognition
Sharvari’s defining year came in **2024** with the 100-crore blockbuster **MUNJYA**, where she received widespread appreciation, including for the popular track *Taras*. The same year, she appeared in YRF’s globally successful streaming film **MAHARAJ**, and delivered a powerful performance in the action thriller **VEDAA (2024)**.

## Expanding Cinematic Universes
Sharvari is the only actor of her generation to be part of two of Hindi cinema’s biggest universes:
- **YRF Spy Universe** (ALPHA – 2026)
- **Maddock Horror Comedy Universe** (MUNJYA – 2024)

She will star alongside Alia Bhatt in **ALPHA (17 April 2026)** and is also signed for projects with **Imtiaz Ali**, **Sooraj Barjatya**, and **Ali Abbas Zafar**, including a YRF-backed action romance opposite Ahaan Panday.

## Brand Presence and Cultural Impact
Beyond films, Sharvari is a brand ambassador for Wellbeing Nutrition, Nykaa, Sheba, Waman Hari Pethe Jewellers, Sprite, and Godrej Professional. She is also passionate about photography and uses her social media presence as a creative platform, establishing herself as a fashion-forward and contemporary youth icon.
