## Anushka Sharma – Profile

## Career Overview
Anushka Sharma represents self-made success in Indian cinema. With no film lineage, she has built a distinguished career as both an actor and producer, redefining the portrayal of women in mainstream Hindi cinema.

She is the leading lady of multiple global blockbusters including **SULTAN (2016)**, **PK (2014)** and **SANJU (2018)**, making her the only actress with three 300-crore-plus films.

## Acting Career
Anushka made her debut in **RAB NE BANA DI JODI (2008)** opposite Shah Rukh Khan. Since then, she has worked with leading filmmakers and delivered acclaimed performances in **BAND BAAJA BAARAAT (2010)**, **JAB TAK HAI JAAN (2012)**, **AE DIL HAI MUSHKIL (2016)** and **ZERO (2018)**.

Her performances are marked by strength, realism, and emotional depth, often challenging stereotypical female roles.

## Producer and Entrepreneur
At the age of 25, Anushka co-founded **Clean Slate Filmz**, focusing on bold, unconventional storytelling. As a producer, she has backed critically acclaimed projects such as:
- **NH10 (2015)**
- **PHILLAURI (2017)**
- **PARI (2018)**
- **PAATAL LOK (2020)**
- **BULBBUL (2020)**

These projects have significantly influenced India’s OTT and independent cinema landscape.

## Cultural and Brand Influence
Anushka is a major cultural influencer and brand ambassador for leading global and Indian brands including L’Oréal, Puma, Myntra, Michael Kors, Giva, and Slurrp Farm. She is also associated with the **Swachh Bharat Abhiyan**, representing the campaign alongside Amitabh Bachchan.

Her entrepreneurial venture **NUSH**, a women’s apparel brand, reflects her focus on youth-centric fashion and accessibility.

## Personal Background
Born in Ayodhya in 1988, Anushka is the daughter of a retired army officer, with discipline and resilience shaping her formative years. She studied at Mount Carmel College, Bangalore, before entering modeling and later films.

She is an animal rights advocate and supports causes related to animal welfare and women’s empowerment. She is the first Indian celebrity to have a statue at **Madame Tussauds Singapore**.

## Upcoming Work
Anushka will be seen in **CHAKDA XPRESS**, a biographical sports drama inspired by the life of Indian cricketer Jhulan Goswami.
