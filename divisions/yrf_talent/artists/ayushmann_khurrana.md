## Ayushmann Khurrana – Profile

## Career Overview
Ayushmann Khurrana is one of the most influential actors of contemporary Indian cinema, widely regarded as the poster boy of progressive, socially conscious storytelling. Through his choice of roles, he has redefined mainstream stardom by portraying vulnerable, grounded, and flawed protagonists rooted in India’s heartland.

His films consistently challenge taboos, encourage dialogue, and contribute to cultural change, making him both a commercial success and a thought leader in modern Hindi cinema.

## Early Career and Entry into Films
Before acting, Ayushmann worked as a radio jockey with Big FM and a video jockey with MTV, showcasing his versatility early on. He made his film debut in **VICKY DONOR (2012)**, a taboo-breaking comedy where he played a sperm donor. The film was a critical and commercial success and earned him the **Filmfare Award for Best Male Debut**.

## Defining Filmography and Critical Acclaim
Ayushmann’s performances in films such as **DUM LAGA KE HAISHA (2015)**, **BAREILLY KI BARFI (2017)**, **BADHAAI HO (2018)** and **ARTICLE 15 (2019)** redefined the idea of a Hindi film hero.  
His performance in **ANDHADHUN (2018)** won him the **National Film Award**, cementing his position as a leading actor of substance.

He continued to break gender and societal stereotypes with films like:
- **SHUBH MANGAL ZYADA SAAVDHAN (2020)** – mainstream LGBTQ+ love story  
- **CHANDIGARH KARE AASHIQUI (2021)** – first mainstream Bollywood love story with a trans character  
- **DOCTOR G (2022)** – addressing gender roles in medicine  

## Recent Work and Future Projects
In **2023**, **DREAM GIRL 2** emerged as his biggest box-office opener. He entered the horror-comedy genre with **THAMMA (2025)**, expanding the Maddock Horror Comedy Universe.

He has upcoming projects with filmmaker **Sooraj Barjatya**, where he steps into the iconic character *Prem*, and the sequel to **PATI PATNI AUR WOH**, alongside Sara Ali Khan and Wamiqa Gabbi.

## Music, AI & Cultural Impact
Ayushmann is also an accomplished singer and musician. He signed a global recording deal with **Warner Music India** and released hits such as *Jachdi* and *The Heartbreak Chhora*. He became the **first mainstream Bollywood artist to feature an AI-generated music video**.

## Social Impact and Global Recognition
Ayushmann is UNICEF’s National Ambassador for child rights and actively supports global initiatives like Ending Violence Against Children (EVAC). His influence extends internationally:
- TIME Magazine’s **Most Influential People (2020)**
- **TIME100 Impact Award (2023)**
- First-ever Brand Ambassador of **FICCI Frames (2025)**
- Invited member of the **Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences (Oscars)**
