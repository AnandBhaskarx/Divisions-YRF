## Vaani Kapoor – Profile

## Career Overview
Vaani Kapoor is known for her elegance, restraint, and quietly assured screen presence. A performer who lets her work speak for itself, Vaani represents a modern generation of actors who balance commercial cinema with performance-driven roles.

## Early Life and Entry into Cinema
Born and raised in Delhi, Vaani nurtured a strong aspiration to act from an early age. With her striking features and tall frame, she transitioned smoothly into modelling and became an Elite model before moving to acting. She worked briefly in television before being signed by Yash Raj Films, marking the beginning of her professional film career.

## Film Debut and Early Success
Vaani made her Hindi film debut with **SHUDDH DESI ROMANCE (2013)**, where she immediately impressed critics and audiences alike with her natural performance and ease on screen. Her second film, **AAHA KALYANAM (2014)**, further showcased her dedication as she learnt Tamil and Telugu to deliver authentic performances.

## Versatility and Notable Performances
Vaani gained wide appreciation for her role in **BEFIKRE (2016)**, directed by Aditya Chopra, where she portrayed a carefree, independent Parisian woman opposite Ranveer Singh. She followed this with high-profile projects such as **WAR (2019)**, where her screen presence stood out in one of the biggest blockbusters of Indian cinema.

She received praise for her performance in **CHANDIGARH KARE AASHIQUI (2021)**, playing a character representing the transgender community, and for her role in **SHAMSHERA (2022)** as a travelling performer. These choices reinforced her reputation for selecting challenging and meaningful roles.

## Recent and Upcoming Work
Vaani starred opposite Akshay Kumar in **KHEL KHEL MEIN (2024)** and alongside Ajay Devgn in **RAID 2 (2025)**, both of which were well received. She also made her streaming debut with YRF’s globally trending series **MANDALA MURDERS (2025)**.

She will next be seen in **SARVAGUNN SAMPANNA (Upcoming)**, a social comedy where she plays the lead, and **BADTAMEEZ GILL (Upcoming)**, a modern dramedy set between Bareilly and London.

## Films as an Actor
