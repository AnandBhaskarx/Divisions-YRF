## YRF Talent – Overview

## Division Mandate
YRF Talent is the talent management division of Yash Raj Films (YRF). The division serves as the sole and exclusive representative for a curated roster of artists across the entertainment ecosystem.

## Scope of Representation
YRF Talent manages and represents artists across all professional touchpoints, including:
- Feature films  
- Brand endorsements  
- Live events and appearances  
- Digital collaborations  
- Personal brand strategy and public relations  

The division operates as a single-window interface for partners seeking talent representation backed by YRF’s creative and institutional strength.

## Integrated Talent Management Approach
YRF Talent follows an integrated approach to:
- Image building and perception management  
- Strategic career planning for artists  
- Brand alignment and positioning  
- Delivering customized, transparent, and efficient solutions to partners  

All talent representation is driven by YRF’s long-standing creative expertise and industry credibility.

## Legacy of Talent Development
YRF has a proven track record of discovering, grooming, launching, and building some of the most influential names in the Indian entertainment industry. This legacy continues through YRF Talent, reinforcing the studio’s role as a talent powerhouse.
