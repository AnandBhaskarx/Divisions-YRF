## YRF Entertainment

Division Identity
YRF Entertainment is the digital content division of Yash Raj Films, dedicated to the creation of original films and series developed specifically for the streaming ecosystem. The division focuses on powerful, story-driven content designed for Indian and international audiences.

Vision and Mandate
The division’s vision is to develop content that transcends geographic boundaries and language barriers, while retaining strong narrative and cultural authenticity. YRF Entertainment operates with a streaming-first mindset, adapting storytelling formats to suit global digital platforms.

Strategic Role within YRF
YRF Entertainment expands Yash Raj Films’ creative footprint beyond theatrical cinema into long-form digital storytelling. It serves as the studio’s primary vehicle for:

- Streaming originals
- Global platform collaborations
- Experimentation with format, genre, and narrative depth
- Cross-cultural creative partnerships

Streaming Debut and Early Projects
In 2023, YRF Entertainment made its streaming debut through a global collaboration with Netflix, releasing **The Romantics** on 14 February 2023. The four-part documentary series celebrates filmmaker Yash Chopra and examines over five decades of Yash Raj Films’ cultural impact on Indian cinema.

Directed by Smriti Mundhra, the series features interviews with 35 prominent voices from the Hindi film industry and contextualizes the evolution of Indian cinema through YRF’s influence.

The Railway Men
Later in 2023, YRF Entertainment released **The Railway Men**, a four-episode scripted series that premiered on Netflix on 18 November 2023. Inspired by the 1984 Bhopal gas tragedy, the series pays tribute to the courage of railway workers who risked their lives to save thousands during one of the world’s worst industrial disasters.

The project received critical acclaim and multiple awards, establishing YRF Entertainment’s credibility in premium long-form storytelling.

## Feature Films for Streaming Platforms

Maharaj (2024)
Venturing into historical drama, YRF Entertainment produced **Maharaj**, a feature film based on a real-life historic court case involving a journalist challenging the conduct of a revered leader. Directed by Siddharth P. Malhotra, the film marked the debut of Junaid Khan and starred Jaideep Ahlawat, Shalini Pandey, and Sharvari (special appearance). Maharaj premiered on Netflix on 21 June 2024.

Vijay 69 (2024)
Continuing its expansion into character-driven narratives, the division released **Vijay 69** on Netflix on 8 November 2024. Directed by Akshay Roy and starring Anupam Kher, the film presents a slice-of-life story about a man who decides to compete in a triathlon at the age of 69, exploring themes of resilience, self-belief, and second chances.

Series Expansion and Genre Diversification

Mandala Murders (2025)
In 2025, YRF Entertainment expanded into high-concept thrillers with **Mandala Murders**, a suspense-driven series starring Vaani Kapoor, Vaibhav Raj Gupta, and Surveen Chawla. Directed by Gopi Puthran and Manan Rawat, the series premiered on Netflix on 25 July 2025.

Akka (Upcoming – 2026)
The division’s upcoming series **Akka** is an ambitious period drama set in the 1980s within the fictional matriarchal city of Pernuru. Written and directed by Dharmaraj Shetty, the series stars Keerthy Suresh, Radhika Apte, and Tanvi Azmi. Akka explores power, survival, and internal conflict within a society ruled by formidable gangster-queens and is scheduled for release on Netflix in 2026.

## International and Cross-Cultural Collaborations

YRF Entertainment actively facilitates cross-cultural collaboration between its Mumbai-based studio and US-based writers, directors, and technicians. The division also develops and produces English-language films as part of its global content strategy.

English-Language Productions
At its inception, YRF Entertainment financed and produced **The Longest Week**, starring Jason Bateman and Olivia Wilde, available on Amazon Prime. This was followed by **Grace of Monaco**, starring Nicole Kidman, which opened the Cannes Film Festival and is also available on Amazon Prime.

The division has further developed **Seducing Ingrid Bergman**, a blacklist screenplay written by Arash Amel, based on the relationship between Ingrid Bergman and photographer Robert Capa.

Positioning and Creative Philosophy
YRF Entertainment positions itself as a premium content studio focused on:

- Story-first development
- Creator-driven projects
- Long-form narrative depth
- Global relevance with Indian storytelling sensibilities
