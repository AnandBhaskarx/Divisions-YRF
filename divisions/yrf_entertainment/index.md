## YRF Entertainment

### Division Summary
YRF Entertainment is the digital content division of Yash Raj Films, focused on developing and producing original films and series for global streaming platforms.

### Core Focus Areas
- Streaming-first films and series
- Long-form episodic storytelling
- Documentary and non-fiction content
- International and cross-cultural collaborations

### Content Formats
- Scripted series
- Feature-length films
- Documentary series
- English-language productions

### Platform Orientation
- Global streaming platforms
- India-first stories with international reach
