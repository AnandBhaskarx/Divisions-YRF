## YRF Merchandising

Division Identity
YRF Merchandising is the division of Yash Raj Films dedicated to developing and managing film-based merchandise and memorabilia inspired by the studio’s characters, stories, and cinematic universes.

Cultural Foundation
Characters created by Yash Raj Films have been deeply etched in public memory, defined by their personalities, iconic dialogues, costumes, and visual identities. These elements form the creative foundation of the merchandising division’s work.

Strategic Mandate
The division’s mandate is to extend the emotional and cultural connection audiences have with YRF films beyond the screen. This is achieved by transforming memorable cinematic elements into tangible products that allow audiences to engage with films long after their theatrical or digital release.

Industry Pioneering Role
YRF Merchandising has played a pioneering role in developing the niche segment of film merchandising within the Indian entertainment industry. The division identified early opportunities to create licensed products rooted in storytelling and character identity rather than generic branding.

Merchandise Philosophy
The merchandising strategy focuses on:

- Authentic representation of film characters
- Faithful adaptation of costumes, symbols, and visual motifs
- High recall value tied to iconic moments and personas
- Emotional resonance with cinema audiences

Global Audience Engagement
Through its merchandise offerings, the division enables cinema lovers across the world to cherish and own a piece of YRF’s cinematic legacy. This approach strengthens brand loyalty and reinforces YRF’s global cultural footprint.

Integration with Studio Ecosystem
YRF Merchandising operates in alignment with film production, marketing, and brand strategy teams to ensure consistency in:

- Character portrayal
- Visual identity
- Narrative authenticity
- Brand integrity
