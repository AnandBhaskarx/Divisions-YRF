## Film Distribution (India)

Yash Raj Films (YRF) maintains internally owned distribution companies across the Indian subcontinent, establishing itself as a premier distributor of feature films. The division focuses on:

- **Process Innovation**: Implementing new-age, process-driven practices to set industry benchmarks in revenue.
- **Business Ethics**: Ensuring fair business practices across all domestic circuits.
- **Market Reach**: Total coverage of the Indian territory through specialized distribution teams.

In addition to home productions, YRF distributes content from other renowned filmmakers.

- **Key Internal Releases**: _WAR 2_, _TIGER 3_, _PATHAAN_, _DHOOM:3_, _DILWALE DULHANIA LE JAYENGE_, and the _Spy Universe_ films.
- **Third-Party Releases**: Successful distribution of external films such as _TU JHOOTHI MAIN MAKKAAR_.

YRF aggressively targets regional markets by releasing major titles in multiple Indian languages:

- **Primary Languages**: Hindi, Tamil, Telugu.
- **Regional Languages**: Bengali (_GUNDAY_), Punjabi (_YAARAN NAAL BAHARAN_), and English (_GRACE OF MONACO_).
