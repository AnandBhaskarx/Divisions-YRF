## yfx studios

Division Identity
yFX is the visual effects facility of Yash Raj Films, established in 2016 at YRF Studios. The division was founded by a core group of experienced, award-winning VFX supervisors, technical directors, and artists to serve as a fully integrated creative and technical partner for filmmakers.

Mandate and Scope
yFX operates as a state-of-the-art visual effects studio designed to support the filmmaking process end-to-end. Its mandate extends beyond execution, positioning the division as a creative think tank that engages with projects from early ideation through final delivery.

Integrated Creative Model
The studio functions as a one-stop visual effects facility, addressing multiple aspects of filmmaking under a single roof. This integrated approach enables:

- Early creative input during script and concept stages
- Technical feasibility assessment
- Cost-effective and efficient execution
- Close collaboration between filmmakers and VFX teams

Core Capabilities
yFX delivers a comprehensive range of visual effects and animation services, including:

- 3D asset creation
- Character and environment animation
- Matte painting
- Compositing
- Visual effects shot production
- CGI virtual environments
- Crowd simulation in both 3D and live-action contexts
- Digital prosthetics

Technology Infrastructure
The division operates on industry-standard, state-of-the-art technology pipelines, including:

- Autodesk Maya
- SideFX Houdini
- Foundry Nuke
- Autodesk Flame

These pipelines are supported by high-speed servers and a large render farm, enabling the delivery of high-volume, effects-intensive productions.

First Major Project
yFX’s first major assignment was the visual effects work for **Sultan** (2016), starring Salman Khan. The project involved:

- CGI virtual sets
- Extensive crowd multiplication
- Integration of 3D and live crowd elements
- Digital prosthetics

The film required the delivery of over 2,000 VFX shots, establishing yFX’s operational scale and technical capability.

YFX Studios: Visual Effects Excellence
Detailed Operational Structure

yFX Studios operates as a comprehensive visual effects facility integrated within the YRF ecosystem while maintaining capabilities competitive with leading global VFX houses. The studio combines creative direction, technical pipelines, and production management to deliver high-volume, high-quality VFX for feature films, television and advertising.

## Team Organization

The ~250+ employee workforce organizes across specialized departments focused on delivering end-to-end VFX services.

Production Department

- **VFX Producers:** Managing project budgets, schedules, and client relationships
- **VFX Supervisors:** Providing on-set supervision and creative oversight
- **Production Coordinators:** Handling logistics and daily operations
- **CG Supervisors:** Overseeing 3D work across projects

Compositing Department

- **Lead Compositors:** Handling complex hero shots
- **Compositors:** Integrating elements and finalizing shots
- **Junior Compositors:** Supporting complex shots and handling simpler work
- **Roto/Prep Artists:** Rotoscoping and plate preparation

3D Department

- **Modelers:** Creating digital assets (characters, props, environments)
- **Texture Artists:** Applying surface details to 3D models
- **Riggers:** Building controls for character animation
- **Animators:** Animating characters and objects
- **Lighting Artists:** Illuminating 3D renders for integration

FX Department

- **FX Artists:** Creating simulations (fire, water, destruction, smoke)
- **Technical Directors:** Solving complex technical challenges
- **Pipeline Engineers:** Developing custom tools and workflows

Matte Painting Department

- **Digital Matte Painters:** Creating photorealistic backgrounds and environments

Roto / Paint Department

- **Roto Artists:** Rotoscoping for compositing
- **Paint Artists:** Removing unwanted elements (wires, rigs, markers)

Support Teams

- **IT Infrastructure:** Managing render farms, storage, and networking
- **Pipeline Development:** Creating custom software and workflow tools
- **Quality Control:** Reviewing work before delivery
- **Data Management:** Organizing and backing up project assets

Training and Development

yFX invests significantly in team development to maintain technical competency and foster innovation:

- **Software Training:** Continuous education on latest tools and techniques
- **Internal Workshops:** Knowledge sharing among team members
- **External Training:** Sending artists to industry conferences and workshops
- **Mentorship Programs:** Senior artists guiding junior team members
- **R&D Time:** Allocated time for experimentation and skill development

This training investment ensures yFX maintains technical competency with global VFX standards while developing proprietary techniques that provide competitive advantage.
