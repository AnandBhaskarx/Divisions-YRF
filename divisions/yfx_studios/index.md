## yFX

### Division Summary
yFX is the visual effects division of Yash Raj Films, providing end-to-end VFX and animation services across feature films, television, advertising, and emerging immersive media.

### Core Capabilities
- Visual effects supervision and execution
- 3D asset development and animation
- Matte painting and compositing
- CGI environments and crowd simulation
- Digital prosthetics
- AR and VR content development

### Operational Scope
- Feature films
- Streaming content
- Television
- Advertising and branded content

### Creative Positioning
- Integrated creative and technical collaboration
- Early-stage involvement from script to screen
- Value-added partnership with filmmakers
