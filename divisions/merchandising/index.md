## YRF Merchandising

### Division Summary
YRF Merchandising is responsible for extending the cultural and commercial life of Yash Raj Films’ characters and stories through officially licensed products and memorabilia.

### Core Focus Areas
- Film-based merchandise
- Character-led memorabilia
- Brand extensions beyond the screen
- Global fan engagement through products

### Strategic Role
- Translates cinematic IP into physical consumer products
- Strengthens audience connection with films and characters
- Expands YRF’s brand presence beyond theatrical and digital formats
