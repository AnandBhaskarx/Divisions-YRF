## Section Title
Film Production – Canonical Overview

## Production Capacity and Scale
Yash Raj Films maintains a robust in-house production team optimized to produce 6 to 7 films annually. The infrastructure is designed for scalability, allowing for increased annual output to meet market demand. To date, the studio has produced a library exceeding 80 feature films.

## Market Valuation
Films produced under the YRF banner consistently command peak market values across multiple revenue streams, including:
- Domestic and International Distributors
- Exhibitors and Theatre Chains
- Satellite and Terrestrial Broadcast Channels
- Global Licensees and Catalogue Repertoire Users

## Global Footprint and Tourism Impact
YRF holds the distinction of filming in the highest number of international locations among Indian production houses. This global approach has directly influenced international tourism:
- **Strategic Impact**: The studio's long-standing association with Switzerland, pioneered by Mr. Yash Chopra, serves as a primary case study for film-induced tourism.
- **Pioneering Locations**: YRF has successfully executed shoots in territories previously untapped by Indian cinema, including Cuba, Brazil, Namibia, Croatia, Ireland, and Portugal.
- **Institutional Relations**: The studio maintains active engagement with global tourism offices seeking to host YRF productions.

## Technical Quality and Benchmarking
The studio prioritizes high-tier production values, focusing on "look, feel, and technique" comparable to global Hollywood standards. Key technical benchmarks include:
- *WAR* and *WAR 2*
- *PATHAAN*
- *TIGER 3* and *TIGER ZINDA HAI*
- *DHOOM:3*
- *THUGS OF HINDOSTAN*

## International Line Production
YRF provides specialized line production services for international projects. A notable collaboration includes the production of the major Chinese feature film, *Buddies in India*.

## Future Strategic Vision
The studio's roadmap involves increasing annual output under the YRF banner while utilizing **YRF Studios** to back a diverse portfolio of films across multiple genres. Every production is mandated to maintain the "YRF stamp of quality" and compete as top-tier annual releases.
