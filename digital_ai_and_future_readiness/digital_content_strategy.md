## Strategic Overview
YRF’s digital content strategy focuses on extending the lifecycle, reach, and monetization of its intellectual property across platforms and geographies.

## Core Objectives
- Maximize global accessibility of YRF content
- Preserve brand consistency across platforms
- Enable data-driven distribution decisions
- Balance exclusivity with long-term value creation

## Content Formats
Digital strategy spans:
- Full-length feature films
- Music and song videos
- FAST (Free Ad-Supported Streaming TV) channels
- Short-form and promotional content
- Archival and legacy catalogue exploitation

## Platform Approach
YRF partners selectively with:
- Global OTT platforms
- Regional digital platforms
- FAST channel aggregators
- Music streaming services

Each partnership is structured based on:
- Territory
- Windowing
- Audience reach
- Revenue optimization

## Brand & IP Protection
All digital deployments adhere to:
- Licensing terms
- Content integrity standards
- Brand usage guidelines

## Long-Term Vision
Digital is treated as a core pillar of YRF’s studio model, not an ancillary revenue stream.
