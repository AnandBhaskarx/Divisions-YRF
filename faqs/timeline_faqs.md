# Timelines & Review Process FAQs



Q1: How long does YRF take to review proposals?

Review timelines vary depending on the nature, scope, and complexity of the proposal.

---

 Q2: Are timelines guaranteed?

No. Timelines are indicative and subject to internal review cycles, priorities, and capacity.

---

Q3: Do urgent requests receive faster responses?

Urgent requests may be prioritized based on relevance and strategic importance, but expedited responses are not guaranteed.

---
Q4: Will YRF provide feedback on rejected proposals?

Typically, YRF does not provide detailed feedback on declined proposals.

---

Q5: When should follow-ups be made?

Follow-ups, if necessary, should be made through the original submission channel after a reasonable review period.
