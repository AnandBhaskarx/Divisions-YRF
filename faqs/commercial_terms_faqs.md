## Executive Summary

This document provides high-level guidance on common commercial structures used by Yash Raj Films in partnerships, licensing, and distribution agreements.

---

## Commercial Framework Questions

Q1: Does YRF operate on fixed fees or revenue sharing?

YRF uses a combination of commercial models including fixed fees, minimum guarantees, revenue sharing, and royalty-based structures, depending on the nature of the engagement.

---

Q2: Are commercial terms standardized?

No. Commercial terms are customized based on:

- Scope of rights
- Territory
- Duration
- Exclusivity
- Business scale and risk

---

 Q3: What is a Minimum Guarantee (MG)?

A Minimum Guarantee is an upfront committed amount payable to YRF, ensuring a baseline commercial return regardless of performance outcomes.

---

 Q4: Are payments milestone-based?

Yes. Depending on the agreement, payments may be structured across milestones such as signing, delivery, release, or revenue triggers.

---

 Q5: Does YRF share financial benchmarks publicly?

No. Detailed financial benchmarks and deal economics are disclosed only through formal negotiations.
