# Box Office Performance Analysis

## Selected Film Performance (Indicative)

Pathaan achieved an estimated revenue of ₹750 crore with a production cost of ₹330 crore, resulting in a profit of ₹420 crore and a return on investment (ROI) of 2.7 times. War generated ₹520 crore in revenue against a cost of ₹260 crore, yielding ₹260 crore in profit and a 2.0x ROI. Tiger 3 earned ₹480 crore in revenue with a higher cost of ₹390 crore, leading to ₹90 crore in profit and a 1.2x ROI. Sui Dhaaga, a smaller production, brought in ₹85 crore in revenue from a ₹45 crore budget, producing ₹40 crore in profit and a 1.9x ROI.

## Annual Performance Trends

In the 2019–20 period, annual revenue ranged from ₹700–800 crore, with profits between ₹150–200 crore and profit margins of 21–25%. The 2021–22 period saw revenue drop to ₹400–500 crore, profits of ₹50–100 crore, and margins of 12–20%. By 2023–24, performance improved significantly with revenue reaching ₹900–1,100 crore, profits of ₹250–350 crore, and margins of 28–32%.
