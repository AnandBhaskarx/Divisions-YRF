## Success Metrics & Financial KPIs

Key Performance Indicators

Yash Raj Films demonstrates a film success rate of 70–75%, significantly higher than the industry average of 40–50%. The company's average return on investment (ROI) ranges from 2.5–3.5 times, outperforming the industry average of 2.0–3.0 times. YRF maintains a conservative debt-to-equity ratio of 0.3–0.5, much lower than the industry average of 0.8–1.2. Additionally, international revenue accounts for 25–30% of total revenue, exceeding the industry average of 15–20%.

 Strategic Interpretation

- Higher predictability of returns
- Lower financial risk exposure
- Better diversification across markets
