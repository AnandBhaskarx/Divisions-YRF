## YRF Revenue Streams

Yash Raj Films operates a multi-stream revenue architecture designed to diversify risk, maximize lifecycle value, and optimize post-theatrical monetization.

## Theatrical Revenue

- Contribution: 60–70% of total film revenue  
- Domestic share: 45–55%  
- International share: 15–25%  
- Collection cycle: 8–12 weeks post-release  

## Digital & OTT Rights

- Value range: ₹80–150 crore per major film  
- Platforms: Netflix, Amazon Prime Video, Disney+ Hotstar  
- Deal structure: Minimum Guarantee + variable upside  
- Windowing: 8–12 weeks post-theatrical  

## Satellite & Television Rights

- Value range: ₹30–80 crore per film  
- Broadcast window: 6–12 months post-release  
- Territory coverage: Pan-India + select international markets  

## Music Rights

- Streaming revenue: ₹5–15 crore per major soundtrack  
- Sync licensing: ₹1–5 crore incremental  
- Performance royalties: Long-term recurring revenue  

## Licensing & Merchandising

- Current contribution: 1–3% of film revenue  
- Growth target: 5–10% by 2027  
- Strategic focus: Spy Universe franchises  

## Brand Partnerships

- In-film integration: ₹5–15 crore per brand  
- Co-marketing deals: ₹3–10 crore  
- Event partnerships: ₹2–5 crore
