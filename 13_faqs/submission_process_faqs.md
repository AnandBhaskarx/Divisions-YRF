# Submission Process FAQs

Q1: Does YRF accept unsolicited submissions?

YRF accepts submissions only through designated channels. Unsolicited material sent through informal means is not reviewed.

---
Q2: What types of submissions are accepted?

Submissions may include:

- Business proposals
- Brand partnership concepts
- Licensing enquiries
- Script or content synopses (via designated process)

---

Q4: Does submission guarantee a response?

No. Submission does not imply evaluation, feedback, or engagement.

---

 Q5: Are submitted materials kept confidential?

Submitted materials are reviewed under internal protocols. However, YRF advises submitters to protect their own intellectual property prior to submission.
