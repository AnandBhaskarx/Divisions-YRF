## Script and Synopsis Submission Protocol
Writers wishing to submit scripts for YRF consideration must follow a standardized 3-step digital process.

### Submission Steps
1. **Verification**: Enter and verify Name & Email via OTP.
2. **Details**: Provide personal and professional work details.
3. **The Pitch**: Submit a **Logline** (max 100 words) and a **Synopsis** (max 1000 words).

### Important Note
YRF will only request a full screenplay if the synopsis is of interest to the creative team.
