## YRF Crisis Management & Contingency Framework

Executive Summary
YRF maintains structured crisis response protocols to manage production disruptions, safety incidents, and unforeseen operational risks.

Risk Categories
- Safety incidents
- Weather and environmental disruptions
- Equipment or data failure
- Talent unavailability
- Regulatory or permit issues

Immediate Response Protocol
- Production halt
- Safety and medical response
- Situation assessment within defined timelines

Recovery Planning
- Alternative schedules
- Backup locations and equipment
- Budget and timeline re-forecasting

Force Majeure Scenarios
- Pandemic protocols
- Natural disaster response
- Political or regional disruptions

Communication Standards
- Internal escalation matrix
- Partner notification protocols
- Regulatory reporting (if required)

## YRF Studio Infrastructure & Facilities

Executive Summary

YRF Studios operates a fully integrated production campus designed to support large-scale feature films, digital content, and high-complexity productions. The infrastructure enables end-to-end execution with minimal dependency on external facilities.

Facility Overview

- **Location:** Andheri West, Mumbai
- **Campus Size:** ~4.5 acres
- **Operational Model:** Owned & operated
- **Availability:** 24/7 for priority productions

Sound Stage Infrastructure

- Multiple stages ranging from 8,000–25,000 sq. ft.
- High ceiling clearance for action rigs and lighting grids
- Climate-controlled environments
- Professional acoustic isolation

Production Support Areas

- Production offices
- Dressing rooms and holding areas
- Art department workshops
- Costume and makeup facilities
- Secure storage for props and equipment

Post-Production Facilities

- Editing suites
- Dolby-certified color grading theater
- Dolby Atmos sound mixing
- ADR and Foley studios

External Utilization Policy

- External usage permitted subject to availability
- Commercial rates apply
- YRF supervision mandatory
- Proof of insurance required
