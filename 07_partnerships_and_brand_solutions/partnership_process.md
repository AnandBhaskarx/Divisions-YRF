## Section Title
The Partnership Process & Engagement Framework

## 1. Initial Inquiry & Vetting
- [cite_start]**Selection Criteria**: YRF seeks partners with a clean financial record, cultural alignment with the "Family-Friendly" YRF brand, and a shared creative vision[cite: 2211, 2215, 2217].
- [cite_start]**Contact**: Brands must engage with the **Business Development** team in Mumbai[cite: 1624, 2412].

## 2. Creative Alignment
- [cite_start]**Script Review**: For in-film placements, the internal story team evaluates if the integration is organic to the narrative[cite: 2020, 2021].
- [cite_start]**Customization**: Designing tailored campaigns (TVCs, digital spots) that leverage the film's theme[cite: 2152].

## 3. Terms and Negotiation
- [cite_start]**Financial Structure**: Typical deals involve a mix of upfront fees and minimum guarantees[cite: 2157].
- [cite_start]**IP Protection**: Strict clauses ensuring brand usage does not violate YRF's copyright policies[cite: 2005, 2158].
- [cite_start]**Transparency**: Clear reporting and third-party audits for major 360-degree campaigns[cite: 2176, 2178].

## 4. Execution & Monitoring
- [cite_start]**Vertical Integration**: Utilizing in-house VFX (**yFX**) and editing suites to ensure the brand's product looks premium on screen[cite: 2037, 2040].
- [cite_start]**Marketing Push**: Coordinating the brand's campaign with the film's promotional window[cite: 2051, 2059].
