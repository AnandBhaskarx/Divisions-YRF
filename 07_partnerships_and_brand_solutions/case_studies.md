## Section Title
Strategic Case Studies in Film Marketing

## Case Study 1: Film-Induced Tourism (Switzerland)
- [cite_start]**Concept**: The long-term association between Mr. Yash Chopra and the Swiss government[cite: 1269].
- [cite_start]**Impact**: Strategic use of Swiss locations in films like *DDLJ* led to a multi-decade boom in Indian tourism to Switzerland[cite: 1271].
- [cite_start]**Legacy**: A prime example of how YRF content can influence consumer travel behavior and macro-economic trends[cite: 1269].

## Case Study 2: Tentpole Merchandising (*Dhoom* Franchise)
- [cite_start]**Concept**: Transforming a film into a lifestyle brand through licensed products[cite: 1415, 1919].
- [cite_start]**Execution**: Licensing of apparel, toys, and motorcycles to external manufacturers[cite: 1897, 1900].
- [cite_start]**Result**: Proved the viability of character-led IP monetization in India, setting a precedent for the *Spy Universe*[cite: 1576].

## Case Study 3: Original Digital Series (*The Romantics*)
- [cite_start]**Concept**: Partnering with Netflix for legacy monetization[cite: 1703].
- [cite_start]**Impact**: Used archival footage and interviews to re-engage the diaspora audience with the YRF catalog[cite: 1412, 1703].
