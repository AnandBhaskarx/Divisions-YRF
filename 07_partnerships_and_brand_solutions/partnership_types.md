## Section Title
Types of Partnerships and Engagement Models

## 1. Integrated Film Solutions
[cite_start]YRF offers diverse models for brand placement and integration[cite: 2108, 2123]:
- [cite_start]**In-Film Product Placement**: Organic integration of products into movie scenes (e.g., vehicles, gadgets, apparel)[cite: 1921, 1922].
- [cite_start]**In-Film Branding**: Visible signage or brand mentions within the narrative environment[cite: 1327].
- [cite_start]**Co-Branded Content**: Custom content produced specifically to promote a brand alongside a film release[cite: 2152].

## 2. Licensing and IP Management
- [cite_start]**Character & Logo Licensing**: Utilizing iconic IP (e.g., Spy Universe agents, Dhoom logos) for consumer products[cite: 1888, 1894].
- [cite_start]**Music Licensing**: Sync rights for brands to use YRF soundtracks in advertisements[cite: 1869].
- [cite_start]**Footage Licensing**: Use of film clips for brand campaigns or educational content[cite: 1887].

## 3. Talent-Led Partnerships
[cite_start]Leveraging the **YRF Talent** roster for exclusive brand representation[cite: 1308, 1310]:
- [cite_start]**Endorsements**: Long-term brand ambassadorships[cite: 1801].
- [cite_start]**Appearances**: Talent presence at brand launches or global corporate events[cite: 1802].
- [cite_start]**Personal PR**: Managing the synergy between a star's public image and a brand's values[cite: 1804].

## 4. Distribution Partnerships
- [cite_start]**Theatrical Tie-ins**: On-ground activation at cinema halls during major releases[cite: 1627].
- [cite_start]**Cross-Promotion**: Joint marketing efforts where YRF and a brand partner to share reach[cite: 2127].
