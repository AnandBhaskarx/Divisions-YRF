## OTT & Streaming Platform Partnerships

YRF follows a **non-exclusive, value-maximizing OTT strategy**, licensing films after theatrical windows while retaining ownership of IP.

---

## Major OTT Partners

### Netflix
**Partnership Type:** Multi-Year Catalog & Premier Licensing  
**Deal Structure:**
- Global streaming rights
- Back-catalog and select premieres
- Timed releases aligned with anniversaries and star events

**Operational Model:**
- Netflix handles global delivery
- YRF controls marketing assets and timing

**Strategic Benefit:**
- Worldwide reach (270M+ subscribers)
- Monetization of legacy films
- Brand reinforcement for YRF classics

---

### Amazon Prime Video
**Partnership Type:** Digital Streaming Rights  
**Operational Model:**
- Post-theatrical releases
- Multi-language subtitles and dubs

**Strategic Benefit:**
- Strong Indian and overseas penetration
- High engagement for action franchises

---

### Disney+ Hotstar
**Partnership Type:** Domestic Streaming  
**Operational Model:**
- Premium Bollywood content licensing
- Bundled with satellite rights in some deals

**Strategic Benefit:**
- Mass Indian audience
- Strong family and festive viewership

---

### SonyLIV & Zee5
**Partnership Type:** Regional & Select Licensing  
**Operational Model:**
- Territory-based availability
- Language-specific rights

**Strategic Benefit:**
- Regional optimization
- Extended catalog monetization

---

## Example
**Film:** *War*  
- Theatrical blockbuster  
- OTT release after exclusive window  
- Streamed across multiple territories with subtitles  
- Sustained long-term revenue beyond box office
