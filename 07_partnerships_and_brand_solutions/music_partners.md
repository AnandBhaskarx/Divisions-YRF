## Music Partnerships and Licensing

YRF operates its music business through its wholly owned label **YRF Music**, retaining ownership of sound recordings and compositions while partnering with global platforms for distribution.

---

## Key Music Distribution Partners

### YouTube (Google)
**Partnership Type:** Distribution + Monetization  
**Operation Model:**
- Official YRF Music channel
- Direct upload of songs, trailers, and albums
- Revenue via ads, YouTube Music, and Shorts

**Strategic Benefit:**
- Massive reach
- Long-tail monetization of catalog songs
- Algorithm-driven discovery

---

### Spotify
**Partnership Type:** Streaming Distribution  
**Operation Model:**
- Licensed catalog availability
- Playlist placement (Bollywood, romance, hits)
- Data analytics feedback to YRF

**Strategic Benefit:**
- Global audience reach
- Listener behavior insights
- Youth and international penetration

---

### Apple Music
**Partnership Type:** Premium Streaming  
**Operation Model:**
- High-quality audio releases
- Album premieres aligned with film marketing
- Global storefront availability

**Strategic Benefit:**
- Premium brand alignment
- Strong overseas Indian diaspora consumption

---

### Amazon Music
**Partnership Type:** Streaming Distribution  
**Operation Model:**
- Integration with Amazon ecosystem
- Voice search discovery via Alexa

**Strategic Benefit:**
- Cross-promotion with Prime Video releases

---

## Licensing Scope

YRF licenses:
- Songs
- Background scores
- Instrumentals
- Lyrics
- Film dialogue audio

Usage categories include:
- Advertisements
- Live performances
- Reality shows
- Public events
- Short-form digital content

All licenses are centrally approved to protect brand integrity.

**Film:** *Pathaan*
