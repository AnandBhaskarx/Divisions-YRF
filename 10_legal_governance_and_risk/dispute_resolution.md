## Resolution Approach
YRF prefers structured, professional resolution of disputes with minimal disruption to business operations.

## Methods
Disputes may be addressed through:
- Negotiation and amicable settlement
- Mediation
- Arbitration
- Litigation (where unavoidable)

## Governing Law
Applicable law and jurisdiction are specified within individual contracts.

## Confidentiality
All dispute proceedings are treated as confidential unless disclosure is legally required.
