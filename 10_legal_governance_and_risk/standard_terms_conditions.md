## Purpose
Standard Terms & Conditions provide baseline legal protections and operating norms across YRF engagements.

## Common Provisions
These may include:
- Confidentiality obligations
- IP protection
- Brand usage guidelines
- Limitation of liability
- Termination rights
- Governing law and jurisdiction

## Customisation
Final terms are always customised based on:
- Nature of engagement
- Territory
- Duration
- Commercial structure

No rights are granted unless expressly documented in a signed agreement.
