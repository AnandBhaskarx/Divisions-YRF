## Force Majeure Concept
Force majeure provisions address extraordinary events beyond reasonable control, such as:
- Natural disasters
- Government actions
- Pandemics
- Industry-wide disruptions

## Contractual Treatment
Force majeure clauses:
- Define qualifying events
- Outline suspension or extension of obligations
- Address termination rights where applicable

## Application
The applicability and consequences of force majeure are governed strictly by contractual terms.
