## Compliance Framework
YRF operates in compliance with applicable laws and regulations governing:
- Film production and exhibition
- Copyright and IP protection
- Broadcasting and digital media
- Employment and labour laws
- Taxation and financial reporting

## Jurisdictional Coverage
Compliance spans:
- India
- International markets where YRF content is distributed or licensed

## Internal Oversight
Compliance is monitored through:
- Legal and finance teams
- External advisors where required
- Contractual audits and reporting mechanisms

This document provides a general overview and is not a substitute for legal advice.
