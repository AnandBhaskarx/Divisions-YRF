## Insurance Coverage
YRF maintains insurance policies appropriate to the scale and nature of its operations, which may include:
- Production insurance
- Public liability
- Equipment and property insurance
- Errors and omissions coverage

## Purpose
Insurance mitigates risks associated with:
- Production delays
- Accidents or unforeseen incidents
- Third-party claims

## Liability Allocation
Liability terms are defined contractually on a project-by-project basis and may vary depending on partner roles and responsibilities.
