## Overview
Yash Raj Films operates under structured contract frameworks designed to protect creative, commercial, and intellectual property interests while enabling scalable partnerships across markets.

## Contract Categories
YRF routinely enters into contracts covering:
- Film production and co-production
- Talent engagement (actors, directors, writers, technicians)
- Distribution (domestic and international)
- Licensing and merchandising
- Brand partnerships and endorsements
- OTT, satellite, and digital exploitation

## Core Principles
All contracts are structured around:
- Clear definition of rights and obligations
- IP ownership and exploitation clarity
- Territory, term, and media specificity
- Revenue participation and payment mechanics
- Confidentiality and non-disclosure
