## Contract Framework
Overview
Yash Raj Films operates under structured contract frameworks designed to protect creative, commercial, and intellectual property interests while enabling scalable partnerships across markets.

Contract Categories
YRF routinely enters into contracts covering:
- Film production and co-production
- Talent engagement (actors, directors, writers, technicians)
- Distribution (domestic and international)
- Licensing and merchandising
- Brand partnerships and endorsements
- OTT, satellite, and digital exploitation

Core Principles
All contracts are structured around:
- Clear definition of rights and obligations
- IP ownership and exploitation clarity
- Territory, term, and media specificity
- Revenue participation and payment mechanics
- Confidentiality and non-disclosure

## Dispute Resolution
Resolution Approach
YRF prefers structured, professional resolution of disputes with minimal disruption to business operations.

Methods
Disputes may be addressed through:
- Negotiation and amicable settlement
- Mediation
- Arbitration
- Litigation (where unavoidable)

Governing Law
Applicable law and jurisdiction are specified within individual contracts.

Confidentiality
All dispute proceedings are treated as confidential unless disclosure is legally required.

## Force_majeure_policies
Force Majeure Concept
Force majeure provisions address extraordinary events beyond reasonable control, such as:
- Natural disasters
- Government actions
- Pandemics
- Industry-wide disruptions

Contractual Treatment
Force majeure clauses:
- Define qualifying events
- Outline suspension or extension of obligations
- Address termination rights where applicable

Application
The applicability and consequences of force majeure are governed strictly by contractual terms.

## Insurance and Liability
Insurance Coverage
YRF maintains insurance policies appropriate to the scale and nature of its operations, which may include:
- Production insurance
- Public liability
- Equipment and property insurance
- Errors and omissions coverage

Purpose
Insurance mitigates risks associated with:

Liability Allocation

## IP Ownership Structure
IP Ownership Philosophy
YRF follows a studio-led IP ownership model to preserve long-term asset value and brand consistency.

Owned Assets
YRF typically owns or controls:
- Films and underlying scripts
- Characters, franchises, and story universes
- Music, lyrics, and sound recordings
- Dialogue, stills, artwork, and promotional materials

Exceptions
Certain projects may involve:
- Co-owned IP (via co-production agreements)
- Licensed third-party IP
- Limited assignment or adaptation rights

Such arrangements are governed by project-specific contracts.

Protection
All IP is protected under applicable copyright, trademark, and related laws in India and relevant international jurisdictions.
