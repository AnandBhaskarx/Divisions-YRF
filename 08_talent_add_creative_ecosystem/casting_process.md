## Talent Discovery Channels
Discovery is facilitated through a multi-channel approach:
- [cite_start]**YRF Casting App**: A dedicated mobile platform for global aspirants to submit auditions and profiles remotely[cite: 2978, 2979, 2981].
- [cite_start]**Global Talent Hunts**: Periodic organized searches for fresh faces to lead mid-budget and tentpole launches[cite: 3072].

## The Launch Formula
[cite_start]YRF follows a structured six-step process for new talent launches[cite: 3071]:
1. [cite_start]**Selection**: Rigorous casting through auditions and internal recommendations[cite: 3072].
2. [cite_start]**Exclusivity**: Multi-year exclusive contracts that give the studio primary management of the star's career[cite: 3073].
3. [cite_start]**Grooming**: Intensive in-house training covering acting, dance, fitness, and PR[cite: 3074].
4. [cite_start]**Strategic Launch**: High-budget debut alongside established superstars to maximize visibility[cite: 3075].
5. [cite_start]**Brand Management**: Immediate transition to the YRF Talent roster for endorsement deals[cite: 3076].
6. [cite_start]**Career Mapping**: Selective film choices for the initial 2-3 projects to solidify the actor's market position[cite: 3077].
