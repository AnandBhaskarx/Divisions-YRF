## Franchise and Tentpole Directors
YRF maintains recurring partnerships with elite filmmakers to helm its major franchises:
- [cite_start]**Kabir Khan**: Directed the initial Spy Universe film, *Ek Tha Tiger*[cite: 2761].
- [cite_start]**Ali Abbas Zafar**: Spearheaded *Tiger Zinda Hai* and the mega-hit *Sultan*[cite: 2761, 3267].
- [cite_start]**Siddharth Anand**: Directed *War* and the record-breaking *Pathaan*[cite: 2761].
- [cite_start]**Maneesh Sharma**: Directed *Tiger 3* (2023)[cite: 2761].
- [cite_start]**Ayan Mukerji**: Currently helming *War 2* (2025) and the *Dhoom 4* reboot (2026)[cite: 2676, 2696].

## Emerging Creative Talent
The studio actively grooms new directorial voices within its ecosystem:
- [cite_start]**Shiv Rawail**: Directed the critically acclaimed Netflix series *The Railway Men* and the upcoming Spy Universe film *Alpha*[cite: 2683, 2751, 2783].
- **Sharat Katariya**: Directed the successful mid-budget project *Sui Dhaaga - Made In India*.

## Creative Leadership
[cite_start]Regardless of the director, **Aditya Chopra** maintains final creative oversight on all scripts, rough cuts, and final masters to ensure brand consistency[cite: 3282, 3283, 3293, 3333].
