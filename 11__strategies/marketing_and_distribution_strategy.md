## Marketing & Distribution Strategy

## Digital-First Marketing
YRF pioneered digital-first film promotion by prioritizing:
- Early online release of trailers, songs, and posters
- Real-time audience analytics
- Campaign optimization based on engagement data

## Data-Informed Decision Making
Metrics such as views, shares, and audience sentiment guide:
- Campaign timing
- Market targeting
- Creative asset adjustments

## Distribution Alignment
Marketing strategy is tightly synchronized with:
- Theatrical release timing
- Festival and holiday calendars
- International rollout schedules

## Strategic Outcome
This integrated approach maximizes reach while controlling marketing spend and extending content lifecycle.
