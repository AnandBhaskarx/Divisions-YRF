## Brand Partnerships Strategy

## 360-Degree Brand Integration
YRF operates a dedicated Brand Partnerships division focused on embedding brands organically into film narratives and marketing campaigns.

## Partnership Formats
- Product placements within films
- Co-branded marketing campaigns
- Full-scale brand-film integrations

## Notable Examples
- BMW motorcycles featured in *War*
- Audi vehicles associated with *War 2*
- Mountain Dew co-branded campaigns for *Dhoom 3*
- *Mere Dad Ki Maruti* developed in association with Maruti Suzuki

## Strategic Benefits
Brand partnerships:
- Offset production costs
- Expand marketing reach
- Align films with premium consumer brands

## Value Proposition
For brands, YRF offers high-visibility, emotionally engaging integrations anchored in popular cinema IP.
