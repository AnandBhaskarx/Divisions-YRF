## Business Strategy – Overview

## Strategic Philosophy
Yash Raj Films operates on a production-driven, vertically integrated studio model designed to maximize creative control, operational efficiency, and long-term intellectual property value.

## Core Strategic Objectives
- Sustain high-volume, high-quality film production
- Control critical infrastructure across the value chain
- Monetize intellectual property across multiple formats
- Reduce dependency on external vendors
- Optimize cost, time, and creative output simultaneously

## Strategic Differentiation
Unlike fragmented production houses, YRF functions as a fully integrated entertainment enterprise. Its strategies prioritize ownership, scale, and repeatability over project-by-project outsourcing.

## Strategic Scope
YRF’s business strategy spans:
- Film production and franchise development
- In-house studios, VFX, and technical services
- Licensing, merchandising, and brand partnerships
- Domestic and international distribution alignment
- Data-informed marketing execution
