## Remake Rights
Remake rights allow third parties to reproduce YRF stories in other languages or territories under separate agreements.

## Adaptation Formats
Adaptations may include:
- Regional films
- International remakes
- Web series and television formats
- Stage productions
- Literary adaptations

## License Structure
Each agreement defines:
- Language and territory
- Number of versions
- Creative approvals
- Revenue participation

## Rights Retention
YRF typically retains sequel, prequel, and spin-off rights.
