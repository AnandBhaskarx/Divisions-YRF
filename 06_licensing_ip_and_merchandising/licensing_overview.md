## Overview
Yash Raj Films (YRF) operates a dedicated Licensing & Intellectual Property division responsible for the protection, management, and commercial exploitation of its intellectual property under the Indian Copyright Act and applicable international copyright conventions.

## Scope of Licensing
Licensing covers monetization across:
- Television and satellite broadcasting
- Radio and audio platforms
- OTT and digital streaming services
- Consumer products and merchandising
- Advertising and brand partnerships
- Public performance and live events

## Usage Authorization
Any third-party use of YRF-owned content—including commercial entities, individuals, or government bodies—requires a valid, advance license issued by YRF on mutually agreed commercial terms.

## Third-Party Representation
Where strategically viable, YRF may represent select third-party intellectual properties for licensing, provided they align with YRF’s commercial objectives and brand values.

## Governance
All licensing activities are centrally managed to ensure:
- Rights clarity
- Consistent valuation
- Brand protection
- Legal compliance
