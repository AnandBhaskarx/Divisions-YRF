## Music Licensing
YRF licenses its soundtracks, background scores, and song videos across:
- Films and television
- OTT platforms
- Advertising
- Radio
- Digital media
- Live events

## Dialogue & Lyric Licensing
Dialogue clips, signature lines, and lyrics are licensed for:
- Brand campaigns
- Promotional videos
- Social media content
- Experiential marketing

## Public Performance
Public performance licenses are issued for:
- Hotels and restaurants
- Retail outlets
- Events and exhibitions

These are often administered via authorized agencies such as Novex.

## Special Rights
Synchronization, remix, and adaptation rights are granted on a case-by-case basis with clearly defined:
- Territory
- Duration
- Media
- Usage scope
