## Portfolio Overview
Yash Raj Films controls one of the most extensive film IP portfolios in Indian cinema, spanning multiple decades of Hindi film production.

## Core IP Assets
The IP portfolio includes:
- Feature films and short-form content
- Scripts, screenplays, and story rights
- Film titles and franchise names
- Characters and narrative arcs
- Sound recordings, background scores, and music compositions
- Song videos and audiovisual clips
- Dialogues, lyrics, and iconic lines
- Artwork, posters, still photography, and promotional creatives

## Ancillary Rights
Ancillary exploitation rights include:
- Home entertainment (DVD, Blu-ray, digital)
- Music publishing
- Digital and streaming exploitation

## YRF Studios Assets
YRF Studios contributes proprietary assets such as:
- Production sets
- Sound stages
- Custom-built production design elements

These assets may be licensed independently or bundled with content rights.
